Chaincode Residency

About

Chaincode Labs is hosting a Bitcoin residency program in the summer of 2019 in our offices in Midtown Manhattan, New York City.

There are two sessions:

Becoming a Bitcoin Core Contributor

(~2 weeks) Sessions to bootstrap the knowledge of developers by diving deep into security concerns, adversarial thinking, and approaches to Bitcoin and Lightning protocol design.

Project Based Learning

(~2 months) Unstrucutured time for residents to contribute to open-source projects or work on a personal project of their choosing. Mentors for the duration of the program will be provided.
